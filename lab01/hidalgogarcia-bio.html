Hi!

I’m Valerye Hidalgo Garcia and I’m double majoring in Innovation, Society, and Technology and Women’s and Gender Studies.

My interest lies in exploring how sociocultural structures intersect with data governance, surveillance, and privacy.

Particularly, I enjoy exploring how certain demographics may become “lost in the machine” and the politics of data justice

Outside of my studies, I’m an avid reader, collage artist and multimedia writer.

My work can be found in Figure 1 Digital Poetry Journal and Perceptions Magazine here on campus. Sometimes you can find me watching Buffy the Vampire Slayer or the X-Files.
