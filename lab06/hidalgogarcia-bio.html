Valerye's Biography

Valerye Hidalgo Garcia is a third-year student from Maryland who is majoring in Innovation, Society, and Technology & Women's and Gender Studies with a concentration in data analytics.

Her interest lies in exploring how sociocultural structures intersect with data governance, surveillance, and privacy. Particularly, she enjoys exploring how certain demographics may become “lost in the machine” and the politics of data justice

Personal Information

Outside of her studies, Valerye is an avid reader, collage artist and multimedia writer. Her favorite poet and author is Ocean Vuong, who wrote her favorite novel, On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous.

Her work can be found in Figure 1 Digital Poetry Journal and Perceptions Magazine here on campus. Sometimes you can find her watching Buffy the Vampire Slayer or the X-Files.

When she has time, she also enjoys exploring game design.
